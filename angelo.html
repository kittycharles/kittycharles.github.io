Artist

Carter Angelo

Cater studied at Camberwell College of Arts and graduated in 2015. Angelos work explores the banality of everyday objects and how they can take on a richness that transcends their sense of pathos. Working across mediums, Cater works with film, installation, photograph, painting, sculpture; cement and starch.His stallations are dishevelled scenes aiming to bring a sense of familiarity and close in on those fleeting moments in our lives as Art. The work is conveyed in a personal visual language, evolving through an oddly methodical yet experimental approach. The work seeks to reveal the emotional undercurrents of the everyday: how it feels to be human, to feel love and to feel pain. ""From a young age i have always loved art and one day hopped for a gallery of my own. Now i can happlily say that i have accomplished my dream and am living it. My gallery features artists that i feel compliment te gallery and i want the gallery to compliment their work as well."
